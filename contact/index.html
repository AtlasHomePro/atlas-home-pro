The Process

Three steps, a week apart.

We call you within one business day of receiving the form. Fifteen minutes on the phone — what the project is, what the building is, what you've already been told by anyone else.

Within a week of that call, we walk the property. No salespeople, no junior staff — the general contractor who would run the job is the one walking it.

Within seven days of the walk, you have a written, line-item estimate. The figure we quote is the figure we hold. Change orders exist only when the scope does.
