📖 Service Guides, Painting Tech Reference
What separates a $3,500 job from an $8,000 job. Paint quality, prep work, and customer expectations.
🎨 Paint Quality Guide
Good tier paints: Behr Premium Plus, PPG Timeless, solid coverage, 10-year warranty.
Better tier paints: Sherwin Williams SuperPaint, Duration, better hide, washable, 15-year.
Best tier paints: Benjamin Moore Aura, Sherwin Williams Emerald, designer quality, 25-year, premium sheen options. Beverly Hills standard.

Key rule: Never quote a color change from dark to light without 2 coats minimum. Some dark reds and blues need 3 coats. Factor into quote.
🔧 Prep Work Is Everything
Interior prep checklist: Fill all holes, sand smooth, prime bare drywall, tape all edges, cover floors and furniture, remove outlet covers.
Exterior prep checklist: Power wash 48 hours before paint, scrape loose paint, caulk all gaps, prime bare wood, sand rough areas, protect plants and hardscape.
The Atlas standard: "Professional painters who actually care about prep work." This is the quote from our brand. Every job must back it up. Never skip prep. Never.
🗄️ Cabinet Refinishing Guide
Good tier (spray in place): Tape and plastic everything. Spray paint doors in place. Quick but visible overspray risk. Only for small budgets.
Better tier (remove and spray): Remove all doors and hardware. Spray in controlled environment. Re-hang. Much better finish.
Best tier (full refinish): Remove, degloss, sand, prime, 2 spray coats, new hardware. Kitchen looks new.
Key question to ask: "Is the wood in good condition or is it damaged?" Damaged wood = repair first = add to quote.
🏰 Beverly Hills Painting Standards
Always Benjamin Moore Aura or Sherwin Emerald, homeowners know the brand names and expect premium.
Color consultation is standard, not optional. Include in every BH quote.
No roller marks, spray only for ceilings and large surfaces. Roller is for tight areas only.
Furniture protection, professional floor and furniture protection, not drop cloth corners. This is a luxury home.
Daily cleanup, no paint cans left overnight, no tape marks, crew picks up every day.
Minimum project $2,500, enforce this. Single room jobs below $2,500 are not worth the logistics.
