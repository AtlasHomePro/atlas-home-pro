📋 The Atlas 8-Step Process
Standard operating procedure for every water restoration job. Deviation requires David approval.
1
Emergency Response, Within 60 minutes
24/7, 365. Tech dispatched within 60 minutes. Call back in 5 minutes or less. Customer gets tech name and ETA by text.
2
Leak Source Identification
Licensed plumber locates and stops the water source while restoration crew sets up equipment. Never start drying until source is confirmed stopped.
3
Water Extraction
Industrial-grade truck-mounted extraction removes all standing water. Document volume extracted. Log time in/out. Photo before and after.
4
Structural Drying
IICRC-calibrated air movers and LGR dehumidifiers placed per S500 standard. Daily moisture readings logged at each measurement point until IICRC dry standard reached.
5
Mold Prevention
Anti-microbial treatment applied to all affected surfaces. Free mold testing included on every job. Mold found = AMRT protocol, full containment, air scrubbers.
6
Insurance Documentation
Complete Xactimate estimate prepared and submitted directly to adjuster. We handle all adjuster communication. Homeowner does not negotiate, we do.
7
Reconstruction
Drywall, flooring, paint, fixtures, rebuilt to code and permitted where required. This is the Atlas differentiator. We don't hand off, we finish the job.

Final Walkthrough
We don't call the job complete until the customer does. Provide written moisture clearance document, warranty, and Xactimate summary for their records.

What to look for, what to document, and what to pitch on every restoration job.
💧 Water Category Guide
Category 1, Clean Water: Burst pipe, supply line, appliance overflow. Standard rates. Low health risk.
Category 2, Gray Water: Dishwasher, washing machine, aquarium, toilet overflow (no feces). ×1.2 rate. Protective equipment required.
Category 3, Black Water: Sewage, toilet with feces, floodwater, groundwater. ×1.8 rate. Full PPE. Biohazard disposal. Always Cat 3 if over 24 hours regardless of original category.

Key rule: Clean water becomes gray water after 24 hours. Gray water becomes black water after 48 hours. Document discovery time.
🧫 Mold Identification Guide
Visible mold: Stop drying setup. Containment first before any air movers. AMRT protocol.
Musty smell without visible mold: Check wall cavities, subflooring, attic space. Probe walls with moisture meter.
IICRC S520 standard: Any mold over 10 sq ft = full remediation protocol. Under 10 sq ft = limited remediation.
Testing: Air and surface sampling. Free for customer, we document for insurance.
Clearance: Post-remediation testing required before reconstruction. Don't skip this.
