Critical Path

These dependencies control the sequence. Skip one and the whole timeline slips.

Insurance before van purchase
GL, workers' comp, and auto must be bound before we buy or insure any vehicle. No exceptions.
Bank, accounts, and software before clean launch
QuickBooks, bank accounts, supply house accounts, and CallRail must be operational. No running jobs on duct tape.
Pricebook and onboarding before dispatching techs
HouseCall Pro pricebook must be built and tested. Onboarding packet complete. We don't send anyone to a job without both.
Lead channels before full go-live
Google Business Profile, Yelp, Avoca, Chiirp, and Angi must be live and tested. No point launching without a way to get calls.

Launch Definition

The division is live when all of this is true:

  • Insurance bound — GL, WC, and commercial auto all active and on file
  • At least one van purchased, upfitted, stocked, and road-ready
  • At least one licensed plumbing technician hired, drug-screened, onboarded, and equipped
  • HouseCall Pro pricebook built, tested, and dispatching correctly
  • Google Business Profile verified and accepting reviews
  • At least two lead channels active and routing calls through CallRail
  • QuickBooks configured with correct accounts and invoicing flow tested
  • A full dry run completed — lead to invoice to review — with no breakdowns
