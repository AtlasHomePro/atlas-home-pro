📄 Required Federal & State Forms

All forms must be completed before or on Day 1. California requires I-9 verification within 3 days of start date. W-4 and DE 4 are required before first paycheck. Keep originals in Google Drive under that employee's folder.

🏦 Direct Deposit Setup

Atlas pays bi-weekly via direct deposit. Collect employee's routing and account number in person. Upload the completed direct deposit form to Google Drive — never store banking info digitally outside of your payroll system.

⚠️ Security: Never accept banking info via text or email. Collect in person on Day 1 and process through your payroll system only.
