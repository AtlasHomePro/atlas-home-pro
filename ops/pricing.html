📖 Service Guides — Tech Reference
What to check, what to say, and what to upsell on every common job type.
🚿 Drain Cleaning — Job Checklist
On arrival: Ask when it last backed up, how many drains affected, any recent work done.
Branch line ($119–$549): Single drain — cable first. If no movement at 50ft = main line issue.
Main line ($225–$495): Multiple drains or recurring = main. Always run camera after clearing.
Hydro jet ($425–$695): Grease/scale buildup, roots. Quote before — not an upgrade mid-job.
Red flags: Multiple drains at once = main line, not branch rate. Roof access = 2-man job.
Upsells: Camera inspection +$149, Maintenance plan +$199/yr, Annual hydro jet.
🔥 Water Heater — Job Checklist
On arrival: Age of unit, symptoms (no hot water / lukewarm / noise / leak), gas or electric.
Repair triggers: Thermocouple, anode rod, element, T&P valve. Under 10 years = repair.
Replace triggers: 10+ years old, corrosion on tank, sediment buildup, pilot assembly failure.
Tankless upsell: "Endless hot water, smaller footprint, lasts 20+ years vs 10 for tank."
IRA credit: Heat pump WH qualifies for 30% credit up to $2,000. Mention every time.
Upsells: Annual flush +$199, Expansion tank if none present, Recirculation pump on tankless.
💧 Leak Detection — Job Checklist
Electronic detection ($275–$550): Non-invasive. Locates to within 6 inches. Always written report.
Slab leak ($399 flat): Includes detection and written findings. Repair quoted separately.
Hot spot test: If slab leak suspected, infrared camera shows heat signature from hot line leak.
Slab repair options: Spot repair (open slab), reroute (through walls), epoxy lining.
Red flags: Foundation cracks, warm floors, high water bill, sound of running water with all off.
Upsells: Whole-home repiping assessment if multiple leaks found, pressure regulator check.
⚡ Gas Line — Job Checklist
ALWAYS: C-36 license required for gas work. Never guess on gas — leak test before and after.
Gas leak detection ($375–$750): Electronic detector + pressure test. Document everything.
Earthquake shutoff ($425 installed): Seismic shutoff valve — required in some LA zones.
Gas line repair: Must be permitted and inspected in LA County. Add $200 for permit.
Red flags: Smell of gas = emergency. Evacuate first, call from outside, then call us.
Upsells: CSST bonding if present, Seismic shutoff, Whole-home gas pressure test.
🚽 Toilet — Job Checklist
Repair ($185–$395): Flapper, fill valve, flush valve, handle, supply line, wax ring.
Replace ($395–$895): Running constantly + 15+ years old = replace conversation.
Rocking toilet: Check flange first. Cracked flange = extra cost. Quote before starting.
Water on floor: Could be wax ring OR cracked bowl. Diagnose before quoting repair.
Bidet upsell: Add $95–$195 for bidet seat installation on replacement jobs.
Upsells: Angle stop replacement +$185, Bidet seat, Comfort height upgrade on replace.
🔩 Repiping — Job Checklist
Triggers: Galvanized pipe (pre-1960), multiple pinhole leaks, low pressure, rust in water.
PEX is standard: Flexible, faster install, lower cost than copper. 25-year warranty.
Copper premium: Some BH homeowners prefer copper — quote accordingly (+20-30%).
Staging: Can be done room by room to minimize disruption. Phase billing available.
Permit: Always required for whole-home repipe. LADBS inspection at completion.
GetHearth: Always mention financing on repipes — these are $8K–$25K jobs.
