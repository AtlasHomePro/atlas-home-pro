# Atlas Brief Archive — Curation Scoring

93 archived articles reviewed. Scored against Atlas Brief editorial bar:

**Keep criteria (article must meet ≥3 of 5):**
1. Has specific operator-level information someone couldn't Google in 30 seconds
2. Takes a position, not just "here are the options"
3. Contains concrete numbers from real jobs (not a price range pulled from the internet)
4. Evergreen — still useful in 2 years
5. Not duplicative with another article in the archive

**Kill criteria (any one = kill):**
- Pure SEO scaffolding (e.g., "How Much Does X Cost in West LA — 2026 Guide" with generic ranges)
- Neighborhood "maintenance guide" with no specific structural insight (Bel Air, Beverly Hills, Santa Monica, etc. — all the same generic advice)
- Keyword-stuffed listicles ("5 Warning Signs…")
- Anything about Atlas Home Pro's services (the company pivoted away from dispatch)
- Covered by a stronger article in the set
