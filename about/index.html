§ About

About.

Atlas Brief is written by David Safai, a Los Angeles operator, developer, and general contractor. The publication covers what trades in LA multifamily and residential real estate, what it costs, and what works for which kind of buyer.

The practice began in 1996. Today it holds a portfolio of approximately 126 units across multiple Los Angeles buildings, including two that were developed ground-up and are still held by the builder: the Felix on Fairfax, a 43-unit apartment, and Olympic Towers, a 12-unit condominium.
