COMMON QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked Questions

What qualifications do I need to apply? +
Requirements vary by position. Licensed trades (plumbing, electrical, HVAC) require valid California licenses. For roofing and painting, relevant experience is preferred but we also offer paid training for motivated candidates.
What is the pay range and how often do I get paid? +
Pay ranges from $20–$52/hour depending on trade and experience level. All employees are paid weekly via direct deposit. Overtime is available for most positions and paid at 1.5x the regular rate.
What benefits are included? +
Full-time employees receive comprehensive health insurance (medical, dental, vision), paid time off, paid holidays, a company vehicle or van, all tools and equipment, ongoing paid training, and growth opportunities within the company.
How quickly can I start working? +
Our hiring process is fast. After you submit an application, our hiring manager will reach out within 24 hours. In-person interviews are typically scheduled within the same week, and qualified candidates can start earning within days of being hired.
Do you provide a company vehicle? +
Yes. All field technicians receive a fully stocked company service van with all necessary tools and equipment. This means no wear and tear on your personal vehicle and no out-of-pocket expenses for tools.
Is there year-round work available? +
Absolutely. Atlas Home Pro operates across 7 trade divisions, so there is always work available regardless of season. We guarantee 40+ hours per week with no seasonal layoffs. Los Angeles' climate means demand is steady all year.
