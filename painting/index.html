Questions

Painting FAQs

How long does interior painting take?

A typical room takes 1–2 days. A full interior can take 3–5 days depending on size and condition. We'll give you a detailed timeline in the estimate.

Do you move furniture?

We move and protect furniture as part of the job. We ask that small valuables and breakables are moved by the homeowner before our crew arrives.

What paint brands do you use?

We use premium paints from Sherwin-Williams, Benjamin Moore, and other professional-grade brands. We'll recommend the right product for each surface.

Do I need to be home during painting?

Not necessarily — many customers give us a key for interior access. We treat your home with the same care whether you're there or not.
