The Company

After 30 years of owning and operating multifamily real estate in Los Angeles, we built Atlas because we were tired of calling the contractor we had and decided to become the contractor we always wanted. We operate across seven trades through a combination of in-house teams and trusted partners, serving our own portfolio, the networks we've built over three decades, and the owners and operators who find their way to us.
