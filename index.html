A Statement of Purpose, 001

Built by owners,
for owners.

By David Safai
Operator · Developer · GC

Atlas is the operating journal of a three-decade Los Angeles real estate practice. Value-add apartments, commercial properties, multifamily ground-up, condominium ground-up, and long-hold portfolios, now writing openly about what it knows.

We cover the LA micro-market from Mid-City outward: what's trading, what's selling, what it cost to build, and what actually works for which kind of buyer. Cap rates, rents, concessions, construction costs, entitlement timelines, the questions every owner asks and most brokers answer poorly.

The writing is opinionated on purpose. Howard Marks: “Leverage plus volatility equals dynamite.” We believe him.

Read Atlas like a trade journal, not a brochure.
