A Statement of Purpose, 001

Built by owners, for owners.

By David Safai
Operator · Developer · GC

Atlas Home Pro is not a marketing funnel, it’s a place for owners to get insights in this micro market of Los Angeles, the 15 Mile core radius zone from mid city (90036). It is the operating arm of a three-decade real estate practice, value add apartments, commercial properties, multifamily ground-up, condominium ground up, long-hold portfolios, and now putting its vision and ideas on the Atlas Brief.

We cater to people that want more deep insights on what is going on in Los Angeles Multifamily, Land Values, TOC, ED-1, Affordable housings, financing rates, insights on cap rates, rents, concessions and also the questions I always get “How much does it cost per SF? How much does this cost? How long does it take?”

The writing you'll find here is opinionated on purpose. Construction costs, entitlement timelines, the mathematics of development, the risks of leverage and I’ll also share philosophies from great investors like Howard Marks. One of his principles I live by is “Leverage + Volatility = Dynamite.”

Read us like a trade journal, not a brochure.
