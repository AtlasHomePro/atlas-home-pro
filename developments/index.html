Developments

Ground-up multifamily projects delivered as owner-builder and developer across Los Angeles.

David Rafalian has developed and delivered multifamily projects across Los Angeles as owner-builder, developer, and project manager. These projects were completed with licensed general contractors of record, with David serving as the owner-side principal responsible for entitlements, design direction, capital, construction oversight, and final delivery. Atlas Home Builders Inc. is his operating entity for general contracting going forward.

Project 01

The Felix on Fairfax

A 43-unit ground-up multifamily development in the Fairfax District. David acquired the parcel in 2014 and developed the property through full entitlements, design, and construction — delivering completion in 2024. The building features a Japanese rooftop garden, zen garden, EV charging, clubhouse, business center, and a unit mix ranging from studios to three-bedroom residences. Currently leasing.

Olympic Towers

A 12-unit luxury condominium development completed in 2018. Olympic Towers represents a family-led ground-up build, developed in partnership with David's parents over a multi-year construction cycle. The building comprises large residential condominiums with premium finishes and was designed for long-term owner occupancy and rental performance.
