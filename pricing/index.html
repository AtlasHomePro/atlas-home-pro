PRICE MATCH GUARANTEE

Price Match Guarantee — How It Works

We match any written estimate from a competing licensed contractor for the identical scope of work. No negotiating. No runaround. Here's exactly how it works — whether you're getting quotes before hiring us, or you've already had the work done.

Option A — You Have a Competing Quote Before We Start

1
Get your written estimate from any other CSLB-licensed home service contractor.
2
Show it to your Atlas technician on-site — email, text, or hand them the paper estimate.
3
We verify the competitor's CSLB license is active at cslb.ca.gov. Takes 60 seconds.
4
If the scope is identical — same materials, same warranty terms, same work — we match the price before we start. No negotiating. No pushback.

Option B — The 72-Hour Post-Service Guarantee

Already had the work done? No problem. We're confident enough in our pricing to let you verify it after the fact.

1
We complete your job at our quoted price.
2
Within 72 hours of service completion, if you receive a written estimate from a CSLB-licensed contractor for the same job at a lower price — we refund the difference.
3
Email the competing estimate and your Atlas invoice number to David@AtlasHomePro.com.
4
We verify the competitor's license, scope, and pricing. If everything checks out, we process the refund within 5 business days.

This guarantee exists because we believe our pricing is already fair — and we're confident enough to prove it. Your problem gets solved today, and you're protected for the next three days. You literally have nothing to lose.

What Qualifies for the Price Match

✅ What Qualifies

  • ✅ Written estimate — PDF, email, letterhead, or photographed document with company name, date, and line items
  • ✅ CSLB-licensed contractor — C-36, C-33, or B license, active and clean record (we verify at cslb.ca.gov)
  • ✅ Contractor has a website with real contact information
  • ✅ Contractor services your area (listed on their website or Google Business Profile)
  • ✅ Contractor has 4.0+ Google star rating with at least 10 reviews
  • ✅ Same exact scope of work — identical materials, same work described
  • ✅ Same or comparable warranty terms
  • ✅ Estimate dated within 7 days of your Atlas service date

❌ What Does NOT Qualify

  • ❌ Loss-leader promotional pricing — $59 drain specials, "$99 any service" promos, bait-and-switch lead-in rates
  • ❌ Unlicensed contractors — no CSLB license means no insurance, no bond, no protection for you
  • ❌ National franchise introductory rates — subsidized corporate marketing pricing, not real market rates
  • ❌ Verbal quotes or phone estimates — if it's not in writing, we can't verify it
  • ❌ Coupon, Groupon, or rewards program pricing — we match standard pricing only
  • ❌ Different warranty terms — a quote without warranty vs. our 6-month warranty is not the same product
  • ❌ Contractors outside your service area who would never actually do the job at that price

Why We Have These Requirements

Every requirement above exists to protect you — not to avoid honoring the guarantee. We require a written estimate because verbal quotes can't be verified. We check licenses because unlicensed contractors carry no insurance — if something goes wrong, you have no recourse. We require a website and Google reviews because legitimate businesses serving Beverly Hills and Bel Air have an established presence. These aren't fine print designed to deny claims — they're common sense standards that ensure you're comparing real businesses doing real work.

Price Match FAQ

What if I find a lower price after the 72-hour window? +
The formal guarantee covers 72 hours, but if you contact us on day 4 or 5 with a legitimate qualifying estimate, reach out anyway. We'd rather honor it and keep a happy customer than win a technicality.
Do I need to get a competing estimate before booking with Atlas? +
No. Most customers don't bother because our published prices are already competitive. The guarantee exists so you never have to wonder. Book with confidence — if you find something lower later, you're covered.
How long does the refund take? +
Once we verify the competing estimate (usually within 24 hours), refunds are processed to your original payment method within 5 business days.
What if the competing estimate has different materials or warranty? +
We match identical scope only. If their estimate specifies different materials or doesn't include a warranty comparable to our 6-month coverage, it's not an apples-to-apples comparison. We'll explain exactly what's different so you can make an informed decision.
Can I use a quote from a handyman or unlicensed contractor? +
No — and that's for your protection. Unlicensed contractors carry no insurance, no bond, and no workers' comp. If something goes wrong on the job, you'd have no recourse. We only match businesses that meet the same standards we hold ourselves to.
What if the competitor's price is for a different scope but similar work? +
We'll walk you through the differences line by line. Often what looks cheaper is actually a different job — fewer parts, no warranty, or materials that won't last as long. If the scope truly is identical, we match it.
Is there a minimum or maximum for the price match? +
No minimum and no maximum. If the qualifying estimate is $50 less or $500 less, we match it.
