Atlas Home Builders, Inc.

Atlas Home Builders, Inc. is a California Class B General Contractor that self-performs on its own portfolio. We develop and build for our own account. We do not bid outside GC work, and we do not take on third-party projects. Everything we’ve built, we still own and operate.

I.

What we build.

Ground-up multifamily and condominium, for our own account.

Ground-up multifamily and condominium development in the 25,000 to 50,000 SF range. Value-add repositioning on held assets. Soft-story retrofit and seismic work on our own stock. Mechanical, plumbing, and electrical upgrades on assets we operate long-term.

If it isn’t on our own balance sheet, we don’t build it.

II.

What we’ve delivered.

Two ground-up projects, both still held.

Two ground-up multifamily projects, both still held. The Felix on Fairfax — 43 units, five stories, delivered 2023. Olympic Towers — 12 for-sale condominiums, delivered 2019.

In addition, 30 years of operating and value-add work across a portfolio of approximately 126 units in Los Angeles.
