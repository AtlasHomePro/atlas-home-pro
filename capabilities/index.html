The Model
Why Atlas runs its trades under one roof.

Most home-service companies specialize in one trade. Most general contractors subcontract everything. Atlas does neither. We operate seven trades in-house or through long-standing partners, all under a single CA Class B general contractor's license.

That model exists for a reason. When a plumbing repair uncovers a rot issue that needs carpentry and paint, you don't want to hire three companies and coordinate their schedules. When a multifamily turnover needs HVAC service, a repaint, and a water heater swap before the tenant moves in Friday, you don't want four invoices from four trades pointing at each other over what went wrong.

You want one contractor who quoted the whole thing, scheduled the whole thing, and owns the result. That's what Atlas is.

Who We Serve
Three kinds of owners, one standard of work.
01

Multifamily owners

Owner-operators running small and mid-sized apartment buildings across the Westside. Unit turnovers, common-area work, building-wide repipes and repaints, capital improvements on pre-1978 stock. We own buildings like yours, which is why we know how to work on them.

02

Property managers

Third-party managers handling portfolios across Los Angeles. Scheduled maintenance, tenant work orders routed through a single contractor, after-hours water events. One invoice, one scheduler, one number to call.

03

Family offices

Private owners with personal residences and small portfolios who want a contractor they don't have to manage. Whole-home renovations, deferred-maintenance programs, and standing arrangements for anything that breaks.
