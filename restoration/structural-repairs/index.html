WHAT WE REPAIR

Every Surface, Every System — We Rebuild It

Water damage doesn't respect boundaries — it affects walls, floors, ceilings, cabinets, and framing all at once. Atlas Home Pro is a complete restoration contractor: we handle all of it, coordinated under one project manager, so you don't manage multiple crews through the most stressful time of homeownership.

📊

Drywall Replacement

Water-saturated drywall cannot be dried in place — it must be removed and replaced. We demo affected drywall, inspect framing behind it, install new drywall, tape and bed to a seamless finish, and apply texture that precisely matches the surrounding wall. Types include standard, moisture-resistant, and fire-rated as required by local code.

  • ✓Texture matching — knockdown, orange peel, smooth, skip trowel
  • ✓Moisture-resistant board in wet areas
  • ✓Paint color matched to existing
🏠

Subfloor Repair & Replacement

Plywood and OSB subfloors delaminate and rot when exposed to sustained moisture. We demo affected sections, dry and treat the underlying joist structure, install new subfloor panels to the correct thickness and fastening schedule, and prepare the surface for the finish flooring installation. LA building code governs the type and thickness of subfloor materials based on joist span.

  • ✓Rot and delamination detection before closing
  • ✓Joist inspection and sistering if needed
  • ✓Vapor barrier installation in ground-contact areas
🌳

Hardwood Floor Restoration

Hardwood floors can sometimes be saved with rapid drying intervention, but cupped or buckled boards often need replacement. We repair and replace hardwood sections, source matching species and finish profiles, and use industry-standard drying mats during the drying phase to maximize the probability of saving existing hardwood.

  • ✓Species and grade matching for seamless repairs
  • ✓Sand and refinish to blend repaired sections
  • ✓Engineered and solid hardwood repair
📏

Framing Repair

Wood framing that has been wet for extended periods can develop mold, warp, or begin to rot. We inspect exposed framing during demo, treat surfaces with EPA-registered antimicrobials, sister damaged studs or replace them entirely, and re-sheath with structurally appropriate materials. All framing repairs are documented for insurance reimbursement.

  • ✓Stud sistering for warped or compromised framing
  • ✓Antimicrobial treatment of all exposed wood
  • ✓Full photographic documentation
🪞

Cabinet Repair & Replacement

Kitchen and bathroom cabinets are highly susceptible to water damage — particleboard and MDF bases swell and fail quickly. We repair or replace base cabinets, match door styles and finishes where possible, and coordinate countertop reinstallation. For insurance claims, we document the pre-loss cabinet specifications to support full replacement value reimbursement.

  • ✓Base cabinet swelling and warping repair
  • ✓Door and drawer front replacement to match
  • ✓Countertop reinstallation coordination
🔧

We coordinate internally for all water intrusion sources. Fixing the source happens before reconstruction begins — every time.

Structural Repair Questions

What you need to know about rebuilding after water damage in Los Angeles.

How long do structural repairs take after water damage?

The timeline depends heavily on scope. A single-room drywall replacement and paint can typically be completed in 2 to 5 days. Subfloor replacement in a bathroom or kitchen with tile work may take 5 to 10 days. A full room restoration involving flooring, walls, ceiling, and cabinetry can range from 2 to 4 weeks for larger spaces. Atlas Home Pro coordinates all trades — drywall, flooring, painting, carpentry — under one project manager, which dramatically reduces the total timeline compared to hiring separate contractors.

Can you match my existing wall texture and paint?

Yes. Texture and paint matching is a core part of what separates professional restoration from basic repair. Our crews are experienced in all common LA residential textures — smooth finish, knockdown, orange peel, skip trowel, and hand-applied Venetian plaster on higher-end homes. For paint matching, we use spectrophotometer color matching to identify the exact existing formula, or we provide a fresh color consultation if the homeowner wants to update the space. Our goal is repairs that are invisible once complete.

Do you fix the source of the water damage as well?

Yes. Rebuilding a wall that has water damage without fixing the leak that caused it is pointless — the damage will recur. Atlas Home Pro coordinates with our plumbing division to identify and repair the water source before structural repair work begins. This includes pipe repairs, valve replacements, and resolving window flashing issues or other sources that contributed to the intrusion. We manage this coordination internally, so you don't need to juggle multiple contractors.

What if there is hidden structural damage I can't see?

Hidden damage is common with water events. Water travels through wall cavities, under flooring, and into substructure well beyond the visible wet zone. Atlas Home Pro uses moisture meters and thermal imaging cameras during our initial assessment to identify all affected areas — not just what's visible. When we open walls for repair, we inspect framing, blocking, and sheathing for warping, rot, or mold before closing them back up. Our written scope of work is updated as we discover additional damage, and we document everything for your insurance claim.

What does structural repair cost after water damage in Los Angeles?

Costs vary significantly by scope. A basic drywall patch and paint repair might run $500 to $2,000. A bathroom floor and subfloor replacement typically costs $2,500 to $8,000 depending on materials. A full room restoration — drywall, flooring, painting, trim, and cabinetry — can range from $8,000 to $30,000 or more for larger spaces. Most costs are covered by homeowners insurance for sudden water events. Atlas Home Pro provides detailed, itemized estimates before any work begins and works directly with your insurance adjuster to maximize claim reimbursement.
