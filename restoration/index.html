Common Questions

Water Damage FAQs

How quickly should I call after water damage occurs?

Immediately. The first 24 hours are critical — water spreads fast and mold can begin growing within 24–48 hours. Call us any time, day or night.

Do you work with insurance companies?

Yes. We document all damage thoroughly and can work directly with your insurance adjuster to support your claim. We're experienced with most major carriers.

How long does water damage restoration take?

Drying typically takes 3–5 days depending on severity. Structural repairs can take 1–3 weeks. We'll give you a clear timeline during the initial assessment.

Can I stay in my home during restoration?

In most cases, yes. We contain the work area and use equipment that allows you to stay home. We'll advise if there are any safety concerns based on the extent of damage.

Do you handle mold after water damage?

Yes — we're mold certified. If we find mold during restoration, we handle full remediation including containment, removal, treatment, and clearance testing.
