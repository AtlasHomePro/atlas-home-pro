The Atlas Advantage for Gates & Garage Doors

Your gate and garage door are the largest moving components of your home — and two of its most critical security features. Atlas Home Pro delivers the technical expertise, quality parts, and responsive service that premium LA properties require.

01

Diagnostic-First Approach

We identify the root cause before touching a tool. No guessing, no unnecessary parts replacement. You get an honest assessment and a clear explanation of what's needed.

02

Commercial-Grade Components

We use oil-tempered springs, aircraft-grade cables, and OEM operator parts — not the builder-grade hardware that fails prematurely. Better parts mean fewer callbacks.

03

Same-Day Emergency Response

A garage door stuck open or a gate that won't close is a security emergency. Our team responds same-day to restore your property's security and functionality.

04

6-Month Workmanship Guarantee

Every repair and installation is backed by our 6-month guarantee. If something we fixed fails within that window, we return and resolve it at no additional cost.

FAQ

Gates & Garage Doors Questions

What types of garage doors and gates do you service?

We service all residential and commercial garage doors — sectional, roll-up, and one-piece tilt — as well as swing gates, sliding gates, and barrier gates. All major brands and operator types are covered.

Do you offer emergency service for gates and garage doors?

Yes. We provide same-day emergency response for stuck, broken, or security-compromising garage door and gate issues across Los Angeles. Call us and we'll dispatch the nearest available technician.

What areas do you serve for gate and garage door repair?

We serve all of Los Angeles County including Beverly Hills, Santa Monica, Brentwood, Pacific Palisades, Bel Air, West Hollywood, Pasadena, Glendale, Burbank, and surrounding communities.
